Our Story

Our story begins in a city called Shizuoka, a place located by the East Coast of Japan. Shizuoka prefecture is well known for the quality green tea leaves it produces, and the green tea leaves harvested in Shizuoka have been locally loved and enjoyed for hundreds of years. We at Green Tea Maniac, pride in the quality of the green tea leaves and how they are harvested, and sustainablly, and locally sourced.
